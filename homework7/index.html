EPISODE 0

ATTACK OF THE FRONTEND

When there is no more hope. When
Winter is coming. When the White
Walkers prepare to break down the Wall
and JAVA can no longer stop them ...

He comes - Javascript. Lord of untyped
data and components, he does not give
up technical tasks "to do nicely" and not
to be afraid of calculations on the side
of the user. But he goes not alone, with him
3 of his faithful friends: jQuery, React.js and Node.js.
